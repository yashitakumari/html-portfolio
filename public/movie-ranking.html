The Best Movies According to Yashita.

My top 3 movies of all-time:

777 Charlie

Dharma, a hopeless man, leads his life in ruins until an abused pup named Charlie walks in and changes it completely for good.

Drishyam

Vijay Salgaonkar is an orphan who dropped out of school after 4th grade. Now he is a content businessman running a cable TV service in Goa.His only interest is watching films.Desperate measures are taken by a man who tries to save his family from the dark side of the law, after they commit an unexpected crime.

K.G.F

In this movie a gangster goes undercover as a slave to assassinate the owner of a notorious gold mine.
